Olive & Champignon Spread

Ingredients

  • 250 g Champignons
  • 1 glass Olives
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • Oil

    • Instructions

    Cut the champignons into slices and heat up a pan. Add a bit of oil or other fat then add the champignons. Fry until they are browned to your taste.
    Empty the glass of olives into a bowl and add the fried champignons and the garlic. Use a hand blender to mix everything together.
    Try the taste and add more garlic or other spices if necessary.

Green Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 500 g Green Beans
  • 200 g Smoked Pork Chops
  • Savory
  • optional Potatoes
  • Vegetable or other Stock

    • Instructions

    Wash the green beans and cut their ends. Then cut them in pieces of roughly 2cm. Cut the smoked pork chops into pieces, and if you decided to add some potatoes, those too.
    Fill a pot with water ca. 1-2 liter and bring to boil. Add the pork chops and potatoes and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
    Add the green beans and let it simmer for another 15 minutes. Then add the savory and cook for a few minutes more.
    Try the taste and add some stock, salt or other spices until you are satisfied.

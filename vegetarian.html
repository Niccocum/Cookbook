Pasta with Pesto

Ingredients

  • 150 g Pasta of your choice
  • Pesto
  • optional: Cheese

    • Instructions

    Fill a pot with water and bring it to boil, add salt and the pasta. Cook for the time your pasta of choice needs. Check if they are to your taste and if not add a bit of time. Drain the pasta and fill them into a bowl. Open your pesto glass and add a good portion of it to your pasta. Mix and add cheese if you want to.

    Ingredients

  • 600 g Wild Garlic leaves
  • 250 ml Olive oil
  • 100 g Nuts (Macadamia, pine nuts, other)
  • 100 g Parmesan

    • Wild Garlic Pesto

    Instructions

    Go to your forest of choice f.e. "Pl√§nterwald" in Berlin. Collect leaves of the wild garlic Allium paradoxum. Back home wash the leaves and remove all remaining twigs and other dirt. Let the leaves dry.

    Get a big pot or bowl and add some leaves together with the oil. Use a hand blender to mash the leaves. Add more leaves until all of them are blended. Add more oil if necessary. Add the nuts and chunks of parmesan and again blend it all together. Add salt (and other spices) until you are satisfied with the taste.
    Fill the pesto into the prepared glasses and add a layer of oil on top. Close the glasses.

    Prepare glasses: Fill a big pot with water and bring it to boil. Add the glasses and cook them for a few minutes. Take out the glasses and take care not to bring new germs to the inside.
